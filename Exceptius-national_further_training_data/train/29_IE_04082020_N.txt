I, STEPHEN DONNELLY, Minister for Health, in exercise of the powers conferred on me by section 69(8) (inserted by section 10 of the Emergency Measures in the Public Interest (Covid-19) Act 2020 (No. 2 of 2020)) of the Dentists Act 1985 (No. 9 of 1985), hereby order as follows:

1. This Order may be cited as the Dentists Act 1985 (Special measures registration having regard to Covid-19) Order 2020.

2. The 31st of December 2020 is specified as the different date for the purposes of section 69(7) of the Dentists Act 1985 .
