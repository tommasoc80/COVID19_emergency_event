I, RODERIC O’GORMAN, Minister for Children, Equality, Disability, Integration and Youth, in exercise of the powers conferred on me by sections 41 (as amended by section 75 of the Health Act 2004 (No. 42 of 2004)) and 68 of the Child Care Act 1991 (No. 17 of 1991) and the Child Care (Transfer of Departmental Administration and Ministerial Functions) Order 2011 ( S.I. No. 488 of 2011 ), hereby make the following regulations:

1. (1) These Regulations may be cited as the Child Care (Placement of Children with Relatives) (Emergency Measures in the Public Interest - Covid-19) (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 2020.

(2) The Principal Regulations and these regulations may be cited together as the Child Care (Placement of Children with Relatives) Regulations 1995 and 2020 and shall be construed together as one.

2. In these Regulations:

“Principal Regulations” means the Child Care (Placement of Children with Relatives) Regulations 1995 ( S.I. No. 261 of 1995 ).

3. The Principal Regulations are amended in the definition of “Emergency Period” (as amended by Regulation 3 the Child Care (Placement of Children with Relatives) (Emergency Measures in the Public Interest – COVID-19) (Amendment) (No. 1) Regulations 2020 ( S.I No. 313 of 2020 )) by substituting “period of nine months” for “period of six months”.

GIVEN under my Official Seal,

5 November, 2020.

RODERIC O’GORMAN,

Minister for Children, Equality, Disability, Integration and Youth.
