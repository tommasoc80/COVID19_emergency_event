I, LEO VARADKAR, Minister for Business, Enterprise and Innovation in exercise of the powers conferred on me by section 1 (2) of the Companies (Miscellaneous Provisions) (Covid-19) Act 2020 (No. 9 of 2020), hereby order as follows:

1. This Order may be cited as the Companies (Miscellaneous Provisions) (Covid-19) Act 2020 (Commencement) Order 2020.

2. The 21st day of August 2020 is appointed as the day on which the Companies (Miscellaneous Provisions) (Covid-19) Act 2020 (No. 9 of 2020) shall come into operation.
