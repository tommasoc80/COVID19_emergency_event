I, STEPHEN DONNELLY, Minister for Health, in exercise of the powers conferred on me by section 77(7) (inserted by section 12 of the Emergency Measures in the Public Interest (Covid-19) Act 2020 (No. 2 of 2020)) of the Pharmacy Act 2007 (No. 20 of 2007) (as adapted by the Health and Children (Alteration of Name of Department and Title of Minister) Order 2011 ( S.I. No. 219 of 2011 )), hereby order as follows:

1. This Order may be cited as the Pharmacy Act 2007 (Special measures registration having regard to Covid-19) Order 2020.

2. The 31st of December 2020 is specified as the different date for the purposes of section 77(6) of the Pharmacy Act 2007 .
