I, PASCHAL DONOHOE, Minister for Finance, in exercise of the powers conferred on me by section 12 (2) of the Financial Provisions (Covid-19) Act 2020 hereby order as follows:

1. This Order may be cited as the Financial Provisions (Covid-19) Act 2020 (Commencement) Order 2020.

2. The 20th day of July 2020 is appointed as the day on which the Financial Provisions (Covid-19) Act 2020 shall come into operation.

GIVEN under my Official Seal,

20 July, 2020.

PASCHAL DONOHOE,

Minister for Finance.
